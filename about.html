I am Vivek Sah, a Product Designer from India, Bihar.

I am a Product Designer based in Bihar, India, with over 6 years of experience building impactful digital products. But my journey as a creator started long before that. If you look at my trajectory, you’ll see I’ve worn a lot of hats: Freelance Graphic Designer, Character Animator in Delhi, and now, Product Designer. Why the shifts? Simple—I am a builder at heart. I thrive on creating things, crafting stories, and bringing concepts to life, a passion rooted in my love for sketching and concept art. My multi-disciplinary background spans across Product Design, Graphics, Motion Design, 3D Modeling, and Animation. This unique blend allows me to look at product challenges through a highly creative and holistic lens.

The Journey So Far

The Early Days: While in college, I started out freelancing as a graphic designer for Delhi-based brands. Post-college, my fascination with emotion and storytelling led me into the world of character animation, creating cartoons for children.

The Bangalore Chapter: I transitioned into Product Design in Bangalore, where I spent 2 years shaping a massive social platform that grouped people into interactive chatrooms based on shared interests. I handled everything a fast-growing startup needed—from the core app design to promotional launch videos.

The Global Remote Era: Following the pandemic, I began working remotely with a London-based compliance fintech. I focused on migrating complex legacy software into modern, highly functional dashboards, setting up robust design systems, and giving users total control over their data. (You can read the deep dive in my Compliance Solutions case study).

Beyond the Pixels

Having grown up across 5 different Indian states, adaptability is practically my superpower. I thrive in fast-paced, shifting environments. I blend a visionary mindset with meticulous execution, drawing inspiration from nature, culture, and human behavior to design empathetic, user-centric solutions. Outside of work, I am a traveler and an aspiring visionary who loves stargazing on mountains, listening to music, and planning my next big creative project.
